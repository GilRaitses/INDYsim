## Completed Phases

### Phase 1: Kernel Discovery (Complete)
- 6-parameter gamma-difference kernel
- R² = 0.968 against 12-basis raised-cosine
- Timescales: τ₁ = 0.29s (fast), τ₂ = 3.81s (slow)

### Phase 2: Validation (Complete)
- Rate ratio: 0.97 (target: 0.8-1.25)
- PSTH correlation: 0.84
- Suppression match: 2× LED-ON vs LED-OFF

### Phase 3: Factorial Extension (Complete)
- 2×2 design: Intensity × Background Pattern
- 7,288 events, 623 tracks, 12 experiments
- All effects significant (p < 0.05)
- Key findings:
  - 66% weaker suppression for 50→250 (partial adaptation)
  - 15% stronger suppression for cycling background (reduced adaptation)
  - Kernel shape invariant across conditions (r > 0.94)
